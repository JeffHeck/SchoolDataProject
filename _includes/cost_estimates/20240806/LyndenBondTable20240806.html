Property tax impact estimate calculator for the August 6th 2024 bond measure proposed by the Lynden SD

Input

Output

Estimated obligations for the example property

Notes

This interactive calculator was created by the author independently from and not associated with the school district.

POF: Proportional Obligation Factor equals the assessed value (AV) of the example property divided by the Total AV of the properties within the district's tax area.

Notice how changing the Total AV Change percent guesses affects the calculated rates but not the bond amounts for the owner of the example property.

The one important assumption in this calculator is that the example property's AV rises and falls at the same pace as the overall Total AV.

Equations

There are 2 ways to calculate the property tax for a given year for a given property. They are equivalent.

For any given year:

POF = {Example Property AV} / {Total AV}

Example Property New Bond Amount = {POF} X {New Bond Amount}

Resulting New Bond Tax Rate = {New Bond Amount} / {Total AV} X 1,000

Example Property New Bond Amount = {Resulting New Bond Tax Rate} X {Example Property AV} / 1,000
